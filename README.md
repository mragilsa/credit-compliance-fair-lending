# Credit Approval Compliance & Fair Lending Risk Analysis

This repository presents a data-driven analysis of credit approval decisions with a focus on regulatory compliance, fair lending principles, and risk-based decision making.

## Dataset
Source: [Kaggle – Loan Approval Classification Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/taweilo/loan-approval-classification-data)

Dataset Description:
- Synthetic credit risk dataset with 45,000 records and 14 variables
- Includes applicant demographics, income, credit profile, and loan attributes
- Mixed categorical and numerical features for compliance and risk analysis
- Target variable: loan_status (1 = approved, 0 = rejected)

## Conclusion
This analysis evaluates credit approval decisions from a compliance and fair lending perspective. The findings indicate that approvals are largely driven by measurable financial risk factors, with no strong evidence of systematic demographic bias, supporting risk-based and compliant lending practices.
